Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Stealth Ad & Tracker Blocker is committed to protecting your privacy. This browser extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location or web history
  • Authentication or login credentials
  • User activity such as keystrokes, clicks, or scrolling

Extension Permissions

The permissions requested by this extension are strictly used to block ads and trackers, enable features like dark mode and ad replacement, and allow the extension to function across websites. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

Third Parties

This extension does not share any information with third parties and does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, they will be posted here. We encourage users to review this page occasionally.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer through the support form on the Chrome Web Store listing.
